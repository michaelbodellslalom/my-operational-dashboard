# Project Overview: Case Management Dashboard

## Project Purpose
A real-time case management dashboard for public and social impact organizations to monitor caseloads, track outcomes, manage grant disbursements, and measure service utilization.

## Target Users
- Case Management Supervisors
- Program Directors
- Social Workers
- Grant Managers
- Program Analysts
- Regional Coordinators

## Core Domain
**Industry**: Public & Social Impact
**Focus**: Case management for social services programs including:
- Housing Assistance
- Healthcare Access
- Job Training & Employment
- Mental Health Services
- Family Support Services
- Education Support
- Emergency Assistance

## Key Data Entities
- **Cases**: Client cases with status, priority, assigned case worker, program, outcome metrics
- **Issues**: Case-related problems (documentation, eligibility, service access, compliance)
- **Regional Data**: Performance metrics by geographic region (Northeast, Southeast, Midwest, West, Southwest)
- **Metrics**: KPIs - caseload, outcome rates, resolution times, grant disbursements

## Success Metrics
- Total Active Cases
- Successful Outcomes (%)
- Active Issues
- Average Resolution Time (days)
- Grant Disbursement tracking
- Service Utilization rates
